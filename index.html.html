About Misty Mountain

Located in Can-ayan, Malaybalay City, Bukidnon, Misty Mountain is a peaceful escape surrounded by lush pine forests and cool foggy weather. It’s an ideal destination for hikers, campers, and anyone who loves nature’s tranquility. The mountain’s serene atmosphere makes it a favorite weekend spot among locals and travelers.
